Seminars

How do you attend a Get Slim Guaranteed seminar

 

 

 

 

 

 

To book a seat either click here or use the calender to the right. Simply choose your date and venue and your tickets will be emailed to you.

If you have weight loss concerns and want to talk to an expert or need to chat to other people who are also interested in changing their lifestyle, then a Get Slim Guaranteed seminar is a great place to go.

What happens at a Get Slim Guaranteed seminar?

You’ll be greeted with refreshments by our friendly Get Slim Guaranteed team.

Graham W Price, a successful psychologist and weight loss specialist will talk about his revolutionary lifestyle changing methods that will help you lose weight.

You’ll be taught a unique and powerful technique to help you break through your resistance to weight loss. You’ll learn about our course, though the focus of the seminar is on teaching you skills you can use straight away.

Graham and our expert nutritionists will begin to unravel the truth and bust many of the weight loss myths you might have heard about relating to diet and nutrition.
